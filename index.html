Cinnamon French Toast

Recipe by: https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/222916/cinnamon-accented-french-toast/

The ingredients you'll need:

  • 3 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon milk
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 6 slices bread
  • Cooking spray

Directions:

  1. Step 1: Mix beaten eggs, sugar, milk, cinnamon, and salt together in a large, shallow bowl. Dip bread slices into egg mixture to coat.
  2. Step 2: Spray a skillet with cooking spray and heat over medium heat. Working in batches, cook bread in the hot skillet until browned, 2 to 3 minutes per side.
